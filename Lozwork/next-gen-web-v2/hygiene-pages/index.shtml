Getting the Guardian abroad

The availability of The Guardian and Observer in newsagents is now very limited outside of the UK. However, you can still purchase these publications in a small selection of countries and cities or you can subscribe and have your copy delivered to your door.

For more information on purchasing copies of the Guardian abroad, please contact the international publishing team on +44 (0) 844 543 8005, or email internationaledition@guardian.co.uk. You can also write to them at:
