Matches New 26 Sep

Creating a single page around a match, combining squad sheets, live blogs/robot text and match reports.

  • Friday/Saturday morning – showing squad sheet, with related content (e.g. pre-match press conferences) within the 'more on this match' area at the end of the article.
  • Saturday during game – live blog/robots text with associated stats comes into the article, with the title becoming a live score. The squad sheet collapses to show the first paragraph and can be shown and hidden. Any other associated match content should appear within the 'more on this match' area.
  • Saturday evening/Sunday – Observer match report comes in to the top, but stat tabs are retained. Live blog/text commentary drops to last five or ten minutes (TBD in design), with an option to load more in in 10 minute chunks. Squad sheets stays as above. Post match press conferences are linked within the 'more on this match' area.
  • Monday morning – Guardian match report comes in to the top. Both match reports are shown with four paragraphs, with the user able to show or hide the remaining portion of the article. Live blog/robot text drops to the last match update (with option to load more in 10 minute chunks), and squad sheets stays as above. Any other related content appears within the 'more on this match' area (e.g. 10 talking points).
  • Comparison page - showing all the above on one page.

Notes Show

  • This is basically now one article that changes over the course of a weekend.
  • As per the above, the articles should not live seperately – this is the definative archive of the match.
  • Users in term comment on this event, instead of on two seperate match reports.
  • Tables and scores are left as an archive of what happened that day, with the table representing a snapshot of how the table looked.
  • Match statistic tab should be used as a 'recipie' of what content is required. The look of this should change dramatically in design.

  • Club fixtures
  • Results - the filters can be used to get the Premier League results.
  • Club results

Notes Show

  • N.B. The filters will only work for 'all' and the English Premier League.
  • N.B. A 'load more' option is present on both 'All' and individual competition level fixtures/results to load the next/previous three days/month of data.
  • Competition names within the fixtures/results list go to the competition section/tag page
  • Links within the fixtures go to match previews when available - see match report section for more details
  • Links within the results go to match reports or the PA supplied live text commentary if no match report - see match report section for more details.
