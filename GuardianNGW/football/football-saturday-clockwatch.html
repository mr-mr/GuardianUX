    City's defence remains in an abysmal state. Hazard, with that glorious change of pace, zigzags between a couple of players in the middle and suddenly he's got a free run at the back four. City promptly give the ball back to Chelsea, though. Luiz's low cross just evades Eto'o in the middle but finds Willian, whose deflected shots slithers just wide.
